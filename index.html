Transdisciplinarity Symposium 2022

Dear participant of the first Transdisciplinarity Symposium at Castle Kränzlin,

We welcome you to our three-day event from 20th to 22nd May 2022, with Saturday being the main academic part of the symposium. At the castle, you will be served spectacular vegan meals prepared by our in-house chef and the event will conclude with a boat trip on a beautiful Ruppiner Lake.

Schedule:

Friday 20th May:
18:00 – 21:00 Dinner with salon talks

Saturday 21st May:
9:30 – 18:30 Symposium with individual contributions and a discussion
19:00 – 22:00 Dinner with a cultural event

Sunday 22nd May:
9.00 – 12.00 Breakfast on the Ruppiner lake (boat trip with casual talks)

Location:

Castle Kränzlin is located in Ostprignitz-Ruppin, Brandenburg, next to the Neuruppin town. It is an 1-hour trip from Berlin by train or car.

Full address:
